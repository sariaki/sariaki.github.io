Introduction

During my time at the "Deutsche Schülerakademie" in 2024, I, like everyone else there, got to choose a course. The options were seemingly limitless - with courses ranging from studying law to gender studies - I decided to chose something new to me at the time and enrolled in a course about proving RSA.

This turned out to be the hardest course (atleast in my course's opinion). ... Afterwards, i decided to write my "Seminarfacharbeit" on proving Diffie-Hellman. 1 year after completing the proof, here I am now -- writing this blog post. The following aims to serve as a guide for people to get their feet wet.

What is Isabelle

Isabelle is an interactive theorem prover developed by Cambridge and the Technical University of Munich. Isabelle allows for the formalization (translation into formal computer-understandable language) and verification on proofs. In "regular" proof-writing, problems in rigor can easily go unnoticed (cf.
