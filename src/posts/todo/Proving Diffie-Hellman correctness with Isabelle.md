## What is Isabelle
Isabelle is an interactive theorem prover developed by Cambridge and the Technical University of Munich.
Isabelle allows for the formalization (translation into formal computer-understandable language) and verification on proofs.
In "regular" proof-writing, problems in rigor can easily go unnoticed (cf. TODO: that thing where proof was formalized and wrong). If you see a proof written in Isabelle on the other hand, you can be sure of its correctness.
